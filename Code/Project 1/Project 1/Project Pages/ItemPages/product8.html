Crown of Fire

Creator: Kathia Wenceslao
Item Descripton:
This crown was created for Kathia Wenceslao’s Greek Mythology jewelry line. The line was created for the 2023 Celestial Jewels Showing which was themed Mythology. This line came in 2nd during the CJ 2023 competition that accompanies the 2023 Celestial Jewels Showing.

This elaborate crown was inspired by Hestia the Greek Godess of hearth and fire. The peice that inspired Kathia Wenceslao’s Greek Mythology jewelry line, this crown was carefully crafted and designed to provide an essance of buring elegance. The inspiration for this piece came to Wenceslao during her trip to Athens and Rome.
