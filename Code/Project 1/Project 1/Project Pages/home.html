Welcome to Celestial Jewels


We are a company that designs one of a kind jewels inspired by fantasy and mythology. Many of our jewelry are from the annual Celestial Jewels Showing and the annual Celestial Jewels Competition that occurs during the Celestial Jewels Showing. Founded in 2015 by fashion innovator Euryphaessa Pan, Celestial Jewels is build upon her fasination with fantasy and mythology. Since it's beginning, Celestial Jewels mission has been to provide people with access to ethereal jewelry.

We have ready-made jewels made by world class jewelers in our catalog. However, we also provide the opportunites for custom jewelry. To work with us to create your own design, please use our business contact to get in touch with us.
